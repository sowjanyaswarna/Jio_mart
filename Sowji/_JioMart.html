EDA:Jio Mart Product Items¶

About the datasets ¶

The data is crawled from jiomart website and stored in a paraquet file named jio_mart_items. The file contains a total of 158172 product items and 5 columns.

About the files¶

category:cateory of the product item as on the site sub_category:sub-category of the product item as on the site href:product item link

items:product item name

price:product item's price
